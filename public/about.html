About us

Charlotte Fogh Gallery – Danish and International contemporary art Member of the Danish Gallery Society / Federation of European Galleries Association.Charlotte Fogh Gallery focuses on both upcoming and established artists, working with a range of media and expressions. The artist have all achieved wider appreciation from exhibitions at galleries, museums and international art fairs.The gallery is located in a urban backyard in the center of Aarhus and in an expanding district of contemporary art and design.
