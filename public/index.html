PARALLEL LINES

Martin Bigum, Lars Nørgård, Mie Olise Kjærgaard and Dominic Beattie

01/12 2017 - 06/01 2018

Charlotte Fogh Gallery has the great pleasure to present this year’s winter exhibition, where four established artists will present new works, that can add color and expression to the otherwise gray and monotonous wintertime. The artists’ style and expression are widely different and range from the abstract to the narrative, but with a great colorist vein as the common denominator.

The gallery that keeps you alive

Charlotte Fogh Gallery – Danish and International contemporary art Member of the Danish Gallery Society / Federation of European Galleries Association.Charlotte Fogh Gallery focuses on both upcoming and established artists, working with a range of media and expressions. The artist have all achieved wider appreciation from exhibitions at galleries, museums and international art fairs.The gallery is located in a urban backyard in the center of Aarhus and in an expanding district of contemporary art and design.
